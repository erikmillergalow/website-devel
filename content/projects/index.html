The word Ikigai is used to indicate the source of value in one's life and can be represented visually by a four-way venn diagram in which the regions represent what you love, what you think the world needs, what you are good at, and what you can be paid for. This daily notes app will consist of reflecting upon your day and placing a pin on the diagram representing how you feel you have spent your time along with a space for journaling. Will show trends such as average region and average movement over time.
A web-app that will allow people to join sessions in which they can utilize various intuitive software instruments to make music in real-time. Each instrument will be driven on a loop and the notes and sounds will be manipulated via a MIDI grid and easy to use yet powerful front-ends for synthesizers, samplers, and drum machines. Will support recording sessions and saving specific session setups.
Top-down fast-paced multiplayer shooter inspired by Geometry Wars. Simple gameplay, move with AWSD or the left joystick and shoot by clicking or pointing the right joystick in a direction. Will support massive amounts of customization similar to the fine grained control that the player has over the parameters of each game in Super Smash Brothers.
